FAQ

Is there a way to find someone’s location on LINE?

Yes, there are a few ways to find someone’s location on LINE. The easiest method is using LINE’s built-in location sharing feature, where the other person manually shares their real-time location with you. Are there free ways to find someone’s location on LINE?

Yes, free options exist, but they require mutual consent. The person you’re trying to track must willingly share their live location via the LINE app by using the location sharing feature. Another free method is checking the app’s permission settings to see if LINE has access to the device’s location, although this won’t show the current location itself.

Is there an app that lets you find someone’s location on LINE?

Yes. Spynger is one of the top apps for tracking someone’s location through LINE. It provides real-time GPS data and location history, even beyond LINE’s own sharing features. Can the other person see my location on LINE?

Only if you choose to share it. When you enable LINE’s real-time location sharing, the other person will be able to see your location for as long as the sharing session is active. You can stop sharing at any time, and the app will stop transmitting your location data. If you’re not sharing, your location remains private.
