Method 3: Read WhatsApp Messages from a Cloud Backup

If your partner’s WhatsApp account is backed up in the Cloud, you can conveniently retrieve their secret messages using this powerful method. You can either choose to back up WhatsApp texts on Google Drive (for Android) or iCloud (for iPhone) and read WhatsApp messages from another phone.

Here’s the process to follow:

On iPhone:

  1. Open WhatsApp > Settings > Chats > Chat Backup
  2. Tap Back Up Now

On Android:

  1. Open WhatsApp > Tap the 3-dot menu
  2. Go to Settings > Chats > Chat Backup > Backup

Once a backup is created, log into their WhatsApp account using another device. You’ll be automatically prompted to restore any available backups from cloud storage.

Click on “Restore” and wait for the retrieval process to finish. You can subsequently view their messages, calls and media files without raising any alarms
