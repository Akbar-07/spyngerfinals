10 Signs Your Girlfriend Is Cheating

There are several pointers that every man should look out for to identify an unfaithful girlfriend. However, some are not very apparent and may go undetected for years.

You can learn how to catch her cheating using these ten signs:

1. She’s Suddenly Disinterested in Sex

If you suddenly start having less sex with your girlfriend, then she might be getting it somewhere else. However, there are other reasons that might prompt her to lose interest in sex. Perhaps she’s been promoted to a busy role at work, causing her libido to plummet.

Ensure you talk to her before making any hasty conclusions. If her only response is that she’s never in the mood, then she might be having an affair.

2. She Picks Fights More Frequently

Does she pick fights over small issues that previously didn’t bother her? This might be one of the strong sings your girlfriend is cheating. Picking fights plants a seed of doubt in the relationship, giving her leeway to claim she’s unhappy with you and wants to see other people.

3. She Finds Her Phone More Interesting Than You

If she’s always on her phone and doesn’t make any concerted effort to spend quality time with you, she might be cheating. This behavior could also mean that she finds you boring and unattractive. When my ex cheated on me, she got distant, and suddenly her screen time went from two hours a day to six.

4. Her Drastic Wardrobe Upgrades

When women start hanging out with people they like, they gradually change their wardrobe to incorporate sexier clothes. You may have spotted some sexy lingerie or cute outfits that she never wears for you. Changing her style style could mean she’s trying to catch someone’s attention.

5. She Makes Better Effort to Look Good

Looking good is undoubtedly at the top of every girl’s priority list. But if your girlfriend suddenly starts hitting the gym, wearing nice perfume and making every effort to improve her appearance, chances are she’s trying to impress someone else. Learning how to catch your girlfriend cheating using an app can help you discover who else she’s dating.

6. Lying Often

Perhaps her little white lies have been racking up recently. A woman who tells obvious lies often has something going on behind the scenes. She will often tell made-up stories and provide timelines that don’t add up.

7. She’s Busy on Weekends

Weekends are often reserved for family and loved ones. However, some women will avoid spending time with you by claiming they’re tied up during the weekend. If your girlfriend is away for business trips or getaways with friends every weekend, then you should probably investigate whether she’s cheating.

8. Checking Your Location Regularly

Has your girlfriend been checking your whereabouts more habitually? It might seem like she has become more caring, but the truth is she doesn’t want you to catch her cheating.

9. She Ghosts You Without Any Explanation

A woman who disappears for hours on end, leaves your messages on read and stands you up during your planned dates might be communicating that she’s simply not into you. Chances are she’s dating someone else and you’re just a fallback plan.

10. She Doesn’t See a Future With You

During one of your conversations, your girlfriend could have alluded to being dissatisfied in her current relationship or maybe she craves something more. If she’s hesitant to discuss long-term plans for your relationship, it might be time to rethink your future together. There’s a high chance that she is cheating.
