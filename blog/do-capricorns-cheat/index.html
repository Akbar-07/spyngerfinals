  • He is committed!

Although a Capricorn man takes time to commit, once he commits, he does it wholeheartedly. He is conservative because he doesn’t want to waste his precious time. And he would rather fix issues in his current relationship than start a new one. Additionally, he won’t cheat with his ex because he is totally over them.

  • He values loyalty!

Capricorns are loyal, and they similarly want to be with loyal partners. They are not hypocrites and will remain true to you as long as you’re with them. They don’t have time for games. When they commit, they stay committed.

A Capricorn man doesn’t like games. If you try to pull off tricks to gauge his love for you, he’ll give up and walk away.
