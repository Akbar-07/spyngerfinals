Locate a Phone Number on Google Maps

Google Maps is a fantastic piece of software. Not only does it allow you to seamlessly travel virtually anywhere on the planet, but it also stores your locations, too… assuming you haven’t turned location services off, that is.

Now you must be wondering how to find someone by phone number via Google Maps and the answer is simple – if you have access to the target’s device, be it for a minute or two, you can do the following:

  1. Open the official Google Maps application.
  2. Click on the hamburger menu and open Your Timeline.
  3. Select the date you’re interested in, and you’ll see the target’s location history.

All it takes are these three simple steps!

Track the Location of a Number With Google Find My Device

If Google Maps Timeline isn’t helpful enough, you can always try phone number tracing via Google Find My Device. Mind you, this method will only work if the device you’re trying to track is on your email… or if the email associated with the target device is logged in on your device as well. Plus, the location services have to be turned on and the device has to be connected to the internet.

That said, if you’re wondering how to track a phone number via Google Find My Device, here’s the quickest way to do so:

  1. Launch the Find My Device app on your phone.
  2. Select the email that’s associated with the target device.
  3. Click on Devices in the bottom left corner.
  4. Select the device you’re interested in.
  5. You’ll see the device pop up on the map.

Not only does this allow you to track the location of the target device, but makes it play a sound, lock it, or erase all data on it remotely.

Phone Number Tracing via Find My

If you have an iPhone, you can use the iOS native Find My app to track its location. If you’ve lost your phone, you can log into your Apple account and track your device’s location from there. Not only does it allow you to track your device’s location, but it also allows you to play a sound, lock it, or even erase all of your personal data.

If you’re still struggling to understand how phone number tracking via Find My works, here’s a quick step-by-step guide:

  1. Open the Find My app on your iOS device.
  2. Navigate to the Devices tab and select the device you want to track.
  3. Doing so will open the map and show the device’s exact or last known location.

All it takes are these three simple steps and you’ll be good to go!

Find My Phone Location by IMEI

Last but not least, it’s possible to find a phone number location via IMEI. However, this isn’t something you can do on your own. In other words, this is not a DIY method. Instead, IMEI lookup can only be done by law enforcement agencies.

They can track the phone’s location with IMEI by contacting the carrier with a warrant. The carrier then provides location history from their network, where law enforcement can see which network towers your phone is connected to.

We know there are hundreds of IMEI lookup sites available online, but we’d like to warn you – none of them work! None of them will show you how to find someone by phone number, let alone do it for you.

All of them are scams, so don’t share any personal or banking information with them. Better yet, don’t even visit such sites, since it’s a total waste of time.

The only way to look up a phone’s location via IMEI is with the carrier’s help… and the carrier won’t help anyone except law enforcement agencies.
